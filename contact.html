Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a support ticket?
You can create a ticket through our portal, email, or directly from the "Create Ticket" page. Our AI will automatically categorize and route it.

Our support team is available 24/7 for enterprise customers. For standard plans, support is available Monday–Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.

Yes! We offer native integrations with Slack, Microsoft Teams, Jira, GitHub, and 100+ other tools via API and Zapier.
